YOUTH EMPOWERMENT MOVEMENT zw

Our Purpose

We are a Christ-centered platform dedicated to empowering young believers to become a positive influence on their generation. Our unique approach ensures that both underprivileged and privileged young people receive the support and resources they need to thrive.

Mission

To cultivate a vibrant community of young believers who will live uprightly, embracing the authentic power of God, and reaching out to all kinds of young people, as we flee from youthful lusts and pursue righteousness, faith, love, and peace, with those who call unto the Lord from a pure heart (2 Timothy 2:22).

Vision

To enrich the lives of underprivileged young individuals, adding value to society by teaching Christian ethics and values that foster personal growth, career development, and resilience against peer pressure.

Focus

  • Equipping young people with essential life skills and spiritual growth.
  • Providing guidance and support from experienced mentors.
  • Empowering young entrepreneurs with skills and resources.
  • Reaching out to the community through campaigns and outreach programs.
